About the algorithm

Modified SM-2 (Anki-flavoured). Each card keeps an ease factor (EF, start 2.5, floor 1.3), an interval in days, and a due date. Again: card is relearned now and re-queued this session. Hard: interval ×1.2, EF −0.14. Good: 1 d → 6 d → interval ×EF. Easy: first pass jumps to 4 d, afterwards interval ×EF ×1.3, EF +0.10. Due dates are stored and displayed as ISO YYYY-MM-DD.
